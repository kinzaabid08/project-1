# Chapter 1: The Rise of Physical AI

This chapter establishes a strong foundation by defining Physical AI and explaining its critical importance in the modern world.

## Key Ideas
-   Intelligence requires a physical body for true interaction and learning.
-   The convergence of AI, robotics, and material science.
-   Real-world applications transforming industries like logistics, healthcare, and exploration.

## Learning Outcome
You will be able to articulate what Physical AI is, why it's a monumental shift from purely digital AI, and how it's already creating tangible value.
